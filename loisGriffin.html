Lois Griffin

Lois Griffin is a character in the popular animated television show, "Family Guy". She is the wife of Peter Griffin, the mother of their children Chris, Meg, and Stewie, and the owner of their talking pet dog, Brian. Lois is portrayed as a responsible, intelligent, and caring woman who tries to keep her family on track despite the many challenges they face. She is often the voice of reason in the family and is willing to speak her mind when she disagrees with her husband's reckless behavior. Despite her many positive qualities, Lois is not without her flaws. She can be controlling at times and has a tendency to nag her husband and children.
